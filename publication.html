My research interests are Solar Cities, Urban Heat Islands, and Urban Mobility.
I am a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.
I was a Postdoctoral Associate at MIT Senseable City Laboratory and SMART in Singapore.
My profiles are at Curriculum Vitae, Google Scholar, ORCID, ResearchGate, LinkedIn, and Twitter.
